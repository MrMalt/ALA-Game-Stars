Are you ready for the internship of a lifetime?

Join us if you want to learn super fast and make tons of impact. You'll work at the heart of the Dutch Tech Community in Leiden and most importantly, you'll be doing this together with our talented, ambitious, international and awesome team!

What you'll be doing:

As the latest addition to our Product Team, you'll work on a Frontend or Backend project that will make our products and services more accessible. Chances are that your code will be used by gamers across the globe and perhaps even by your fellow-students!

Next to that, it'll be great if you can help out with customer support. This way you'll really get to know know the ins and outs of our software.

The team consists of five Engineers of which two seniors, based in the Netherlands. There's plenty of room for your ideas here. We're flexible, and as long as you're proactive and take responsibility for your own work, we'll be a great match!

Who we are looking for:

• You have a getting-shit-done mentality;

• You're able to work independently - also from home;

• Also, you have knowledge of JavaScript - Typescript is a very big plus;

• Ideally you have experience with one or more frontend frameworks, like React, Angular or Vue;

• And/or you have experience with one or more backend frameworks, preferably Rails;

• Finally, you're fluent in Dutch AND English.

Shoot us a message, maybe we'll see you in the office soon ;)

Harassment Policy

Game Stars is committed to providing a safe and respectful work environment for all staff and customers. While we’re delighted to help all those who reach out to us through our support channels, we do not tolerate harassment of any kind when doing so.
Harassment includes any behavior that victimizes, humiliates, or threatens a person, such as directing racist, homophobic, or misogynistic slurs at any member of our team. Please note that, should you harass our support staff, your ticket will be closed without further response. Repeated harassment may result in your IP being banned or in appropriate legal action.
We thank you for your understanding and look forward to creating a safe, respectful gaming community with you!
