Are you ready for the internship of a lifetime?

Join us if you want to learn super fast and make tons of impact. You'll work at the heart of the Dutch Tech Community in Amsterdam and most importantly, you'll be doing this together with our talented, ambitious, international and awesome team!

What you'll be doing:

As the latest addition to our Product Team, you'll work on a Frontend or Backend project that will make our product more accessible. Chances are that your code will be used by our customers and perhaps even by your fellow-students!

Next to that, it'll be great if you can help out with customer support. This way you'll really get to know know the ins and outs of our software.

The team consists of four Engineers of which three seniors, based in the Netherlands and Eastern Europe. There's plenty of room for your ideas here. We're flexible, and as long as you're proactive and take responsibility for your own work, we'll be a great match!

Who we are looking for:

• You have a getting-shit-done mentality;

• You're able to work independently - also from home;

• Also, you have knowledge of JavaScript - Typescript is a very big plus;

• Ideally you have experience with one or more frontend frameworks, like React, Angular or Vue;

• And/or you have experience with one or more backend frameworks, preferably Rails;

• Finally, you're fluent in Dutch AND English.

Shipping

If we have all the components of your order in stock, your order will be prepared within two business days* (Monday to Friday, 9:00am-5:00pm EST). Once we ship your package, you’ll receive an email confirmation and a tracking number (provided you paid for a tracked shipping solution).

Insurance

All orders with tracked shipping are insured. If anything was to happen to your order, we will cover the damage and replacements. By selecting Untracked shipping, you agree to waive any claim to refunds for lost, damaged, or stolen goods, whether the loss or damage occurred before or after you receive them.

Return policy

Returning an item

We have a 30-day return policy for items that have been unopened or unused. If you’d like to return an item within this 30-day window, reach out to us through Facebook Messenger, email, or the contact form linked at the bottom of the page.
